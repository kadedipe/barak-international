About Barak International

Law • Health • Wealth • Technology • Logistics

Who We Are

Barak International is a diversified global enterprise committed to excellence across legal services, health and wellness, technology, wealth management, and logistics. We bring together multiple disciplines under one trusted brand to serve individuals, businesses, and organizations with integrity and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be a trusted, multi-sector global enterprise delivering excellence in legal services, health and wellness, technology, wealth management, and logistics, while fostering sustainable growth and self-reliance in every community we serve.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible, and integrated solutions across legal incorporation, nutritional health products, modern technology, digital security, asset management, and financial empowerment — ensuring that individuals, businesses, and organizations achieve their full potential with confidence and integrity.

Our Core Objectives

1

Streamline Business & Legal Support

Efficient registration and incorporation services for companies, NGOs, and legal instruments.

2

Promote Health & Wellness

Scientifically-backed nutritional products (Kyoor, Zarrcof) and therapeutic technologies (Zetta Wellness Device).

3

Advance Digital & Security Solutions

Software development, AI, cybersecurity, and digital marketing.

4

Facilitate Asset & Property Management

Real estate, land titles, property management, courier, and logistics.

5

Empower Financial Independence

Wealth tools, financial literacy, Zara Coin, and Z Pay for lasting stability.
